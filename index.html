About Me

I’m a Roblox builder focused on obbys, maps, and clean game spaces. I like building stuff that has atmosphere, not just random blocks floating in the air.

My style is gritty, black, eggshell white, a little Japanese-inspired, and made to feel sharp. I care about the vibe, but I also care about the player being able to read the course and actually play it.

Respect and love for everyone. Breakups are horrible for a reason, so I turn the energy into building, learning, and locking in.

Build it clean. Make it playable. Add the detail. Keep the vibe. Not every obby needs to look the same. I build courses with mood, direction, and enough polish to make players remember the map.
